The Mustache Game is a celebration of diversion, exhilaration and embellishment. The concept is simple:

  1. Gather group of friends
  2. Choose a light-hearted film and cue it up
  3. Craft paper mustaches and place them on screen
  4. Start the film
  5. When your mustache strikes the upper lips of anyone on-screen, suffer/enjoy the consequences: a sip of lager or other preferred drink

It's a hoot. Give it a try. Make it handsome. »
